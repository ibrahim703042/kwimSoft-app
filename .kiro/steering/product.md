# Product Overview

This is a modular enterprise resource planning (ERP) application built for transportation and logistics businesses. The system follows an Odoo-inspired architecture with distinct business modules.

## Core Business Domains

- **Transport Management**: Drivers, vehicles, stations, schedules, trips, seats, tickets, reservations
- **Carwash Operations**: Service management and scheduling
- **Fleet Maintenance**: Vehicle maintenance tracking and management
- **HR & People**: Employee management, staff, users, groups, roles, sessions, departments
- **Inventory & Warehouse**: Stock and warehouse management
- **CRM**: Customer relationship management
- **Finance & Accounting**: Financial operations and reporting
- **Procurement**: Purchase orders and supplier management
- **Manufacturing**: Production management
- **Sales**: Sales operations and order management
- **Reports & Analytics**: Business intelligence and reporting

## Key Features

- Multi-tenant architecture with enterprise/organization support
- Role-based access control (RBAC) with permission guards
- Internationalization (i18n) with language switching
- Dark/light theme support
- Interactive mapping with Mapbox integration
- Real-time data visualization with charts and analytics
- Trial/registration flow for new users
- User onboarding and invitation system
